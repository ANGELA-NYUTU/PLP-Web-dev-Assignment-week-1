Why Go Hiking?

Hiking offers physical exercise, mental relaxation, and a chance to reconnect with nature. It's a simple way to improve your well-being and explore the world around you.

Beginner Hiking Tips

  • Start with the easiest trails

    • Choose beginner-friendly trails and increase the difficulty gradually.

  • Wear the right gear

    • Comfortable shoes and weather-appropriate clothing make your hike more enjoyable.

  • pack essentials only

    • Bring water, snacks, a map, and a fully charged phone.

  • Have your 'I can do it' spirit

Hiking Safety Tips

Tell Someone Your Plans

Let a friend or family member know where you're going and when you'll return.

Check the Weather

Always look at the forecast before heading out and be prepared for changes.

Stay on the Trail

Follow marked paths to avoid getting lost and protect the environment.
